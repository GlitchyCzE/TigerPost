Welcome to Tiger Spend! Manage your Dining Dollars and Tiger Bucks accounts here.

If you are looking to deposit funds to a student's account, please click on Make a Deposit.

If you are student, staff, or faculty with RIT credentials, please click on Student & Employee Login to manage your account.

Account Management features includes making deposits to your Tiger Bucks or Dining Dollars account(s) using a credit or debit card; setting up recurring deposits and low balance alerts; and viewing account balances and transaction history for meal plans, Dining Dollars, Tiger Bucks, Department Cards, and other RIT-specific tenders.

Students can also request money from parents, friends, or family members using the Request Money feature.
